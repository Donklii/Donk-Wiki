My Experience with Godot

I've spent around 500 hours in the Godot Engine, mostly focused on 2D programming and audio implementation.

Funcionário do Dia

A memory game inspired by old browser games — made in only 3 hours for a game jam, where I served as lead programmer. You play as an overworked office employee trying to survive the day.

  • Engine: Godot
  • Jam: 3 hours
  • Role: Lead Programmer
  • Genre: Puzzle
  • Jam theme: Air Conditioning

Arce Armorum

A discontinued demo following a wizard searching for his magical staff, fighting living weapons that guard a castle. I handled all programming and audio direction; visuals were done by another team member.

The published version is not the latest build — several improvements were made afterward but never released.

Trailer produced by a team member who also composed the music.
